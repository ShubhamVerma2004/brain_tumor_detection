Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is BrainScan?

Our current model achieves 97.2% accuracy in controlled tests. However, real-world performance may vary based on image quality.

Can I use BrainScan for clinical diagnosis?

BrainScan is currently a research tool and should not be used as the sole basis for clinical decisions.

Do you offer API access?

Yes, we provide API access for research institutions. Contact us for more information.
