How It Works

The system processes MRI images through multiple neural network layers that detect patterns and features invisible to the human eye. Each analysis goes through three validation steps to ensure reliability.

Processing Pipeline

  1. Image Preprocessing: Normalization and enhancement of MRI scans
  2. Feature Extraction: Identification of key visual patterns
  3. Classification: Tumor type determination with confidence scoring
  4. Result Compilation: Comprehensive report generation

Our Mission

We believe early detection saves lives. BrainScan aims to make advanced diagnostic tools accessible to medical professionals worldwide, particularly in underserved regions where specialist radiologists are scarce.

Our team of AI researchers, medical professionals, and software engineers are committed to continuous improvement of our algorithms through ongoing research and clinical collaboration.
